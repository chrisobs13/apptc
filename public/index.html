Practice and Areas of Expertise

Dr. Thomas P. Tokarz, Ph.D. A.B.P.P. is the Clinical Director of the Anxiety, Panic and Phobia Treatment Center. Based in Cotuit, Cape Cod, he offers flexible, fast appointment scheduling, 24/7 emergency availability by phone, and can work with your physician, lawyer, or other relevant parties.

Dr. Tokarz offers highly specialized, empirically validated cognitive-behavioral treatment for all types of anxiety disorders. This includes:

  • Panic Disorder
  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder and Chronic Worry
  • Agoraphobia and Avoidance
  • Social Anxiety
  • Specific Phobias (driving, flying, dental, heights, etc.)
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Health-Related Worries and Anxieties
  • Depression
  • Panic Control Treatment for Benzodiazepine (tranquilizer) Discontinuation
  • Integrative Behavioral Couples Therapy

Professional Experience and Background

Dr. Tokarz received his B.A. (Cum Laude) from the State University of New York at Stony Brook. He then received his M.A. and Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. His APA approved internship was done at Western Missouri Mental Health Center.

In 1978, Dr. Tokarz was licensed as a clinical Psychologist in Massachusetts. During his twenty years in practice, he has treated over 1,000 patients with anxiety disorders. In 1978, Dr. Tokarz began his Specialized Training in the Cognitive-Behavioral Treatment of anxiety disorders with Drs. Alan Goldstein and Diane Chambless. Drs. Goldstein and Chambless were pioneers in the cognitive-behavioral treatment of panic and agoraphobia.

Dr. Tokarz continued to train at yearly seminars with Dr. David Barlow, learning his highly successful panic control treatment. Dr. Barlow is the leading world authority on the treatment of panic and anxiety. This treatment is based upon empirically validated clinical research and has a documented success rate of 87% with panic disorder patients. This cognitive behavioral treatment program is short-term (12-14 weeks), highly structured and uses a manual to help produce its highly effective results.

In 1993, Dr. Tokarz became on of approximately fifty psychologists and psychiatrists in the United States to be certified in Dr. Barlow's Panic Control Treatment Program. Dr. Tokarz then went on to become a Master Trainer on a National Level in Dr. Barlow's program. He has Trained and Certified the staff of a major West Palm Beach Medical Hospital in Dr. Barlow's approach. He has also presented workshops and symposiums on Panic and Anxiety Treatment at National Conferences and International Universities.

Currently, Dr. Tokarz has become Board Certified in Cognitive and Behavioral Psychology, which is the Highest Honor that can be achieved in the Clinical practice of Psychology. Dr. Tokarz is on the staff of Massachusetts General Hospital where he supervises Psychiatric Residents and Psychology Interns. He also holds an appointment at the Harvard Medical School and has recently been appointed a Fellow in the Board Certified Diplomat status.
